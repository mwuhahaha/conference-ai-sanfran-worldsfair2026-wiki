With the old hardcoded "PolyShopping"this was fine,but a long backend-supplied title has nothing to stop it-it wraps to multiple
lines （inflating the header height)or squeezes the nav links,breaking the layout.（The Footer is unaffected -there the title sits in
its own grid cell and wrapping is harmless.)
The fix
src/components/Navbar.tsx-constrain the title to a single line that ellipsizes when too long,with the full text available on hover:
<Link
to="/"
title="PolyShopping"
className="inline-block text-2xl font-bold text-indigo-68e nr-8 max-w-[208px]truncatealign-middle
data-testid="navbar-title"
PolyShopping
truncate →overfLow-hidden text-ellipsis whitespace-nowrap （no wrapping,"-"on overflow)
max-w-[200px]→ the cap the ellipsis kicks in at
inline-block→an anchor is inline by default and would ignore max-width/truncate without it
align-middle →keeps it vertically aligned with the nav after the display change
titleattribute→fulltitle onhover,sonothingislost
