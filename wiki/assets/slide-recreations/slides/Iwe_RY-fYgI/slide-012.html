Practical Deployment Considerations
Data Integration
The framework requires structured connectors to payroll, ERP, procurement, and tax systems. Existing data pipelines can be leveraged where schema normalization is feasible.
Jurisdictional Configuration
The normalization layer must be parameterized for each operating jurisdiction. Regulatory rule sets and currency references require periodic maintenance.
Audit Team Alignment
Investigator workflows should be redesigned to consume risk-scored outputs rather than raw document queues. Training reduces time-to-value significantly.
Scalability
Graph computation and probabilistic scoring are horizontally scalable. Evaluation across 3 million records confirms suitability for large enterprise environments.
