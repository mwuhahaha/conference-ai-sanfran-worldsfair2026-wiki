# Evals Are Operational Gates

## Claim
The conference corpus supports treating evals as operational gates for agent behavior, release decisions, and review routing, not only as after-the-fact benchmark reports.

## Why It Is Supported
Evaluation pages, coding-agent workflow pages, and quality-gate talks converge on the need to stop or route agent work when evidence is missing or checks fail.
