# Justice AI Unit

## What It Is
The Justice AI Unit is a dedicated AI team within the UK [[ministry-of-justice|Ministry of Justice]]. Its public site describes it as an interdisciplinary team of AI specialists, designers, technologists, and operational experts working to embed responsible AI across the justice system.

## Why It Matters At World's Fair
The unit is the central organization in [[youtube-qlHaO6laBlM|Shipping Production AI Inside Government]]. It demonstrates a conference-relevant model for production AI: small, embedded teams working directly with users, shipping tools into real operational environments, and balancing speed with public-sector trust.

## Operating Model Signals
- Work directly with users rather than only through headquarters requirements.
- Pair engineers with operational experts who know prisons, probation, courts, and internal justice workflows.
- Use small-product loops: identify pain, build, test, feature-flag, scale, or kill.
- Design for the actual deployment environment, including offline and low-connectivity situations.
