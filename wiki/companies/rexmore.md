# Rexmore

## What It Is
Rexmore is an AI-native holding company that acquires and builds businesses through technology, investment, and education. Its public site describes an operating model where small teams are multiplied by proprietary tools, systematic knowledge, and AI-native operating practices.

## Why It Matters At World's Fair
Rexmore matters because [[corey-gallon|Corey Gallon]]'s highlighted Computer Use talk is not just a browser automation demo. It reflects the operating problem Rexmore is organized around: how small, high-leverage teams can use AI systems and repeatable tools to operate more work without simply adding headcount.

In that context, [[2026-06-30-corey-gallon-the-dark-arts-of-web-automation-teaching-agents-to-use-websites-like-humans|The Dark Arts of Web Automation]] is a Rexmore-flavored case study. The talk argues that browser work should become scriptable infrastructure: write the browser run once, then reuse it as a durable operating capability.

## Public Company Context
Rexmore's public site describes three operating engines:

- Technology: custom-built AI systems and tools for productivity leverage.
- Investment: acquisition and capital deployment in technology, education, and investments.
- Education: a proprietary framework for AI productivity and human potential.

The site also frames its acquisition interest around companies with a strong earnings history and purchase prices up to $25M, reinforcing that Rexmore is not only a software vendor but an AI-native operator/acquirer.
