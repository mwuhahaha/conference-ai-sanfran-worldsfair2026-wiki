## Official Bio
Co-founder of AgentMail (YC S25), the email inbox API for AI agents. The company is backed by Y Combinator, General Catalyst, Paul Graham, and founders of Ramp, Supabase, and HubSpot. Before AgentMail, Adi spent time at firms like Accel and KKR while operating software businesses across accounting, edtech, and e-commerce during his time at the University of Michigan.
