## Official Bio
Amit Desai is a domain expert in voice AI assistants who has led voice AI products at Alexa and Roku, founded startups in customer support AI, and created Top 5 mobile apps in the App Store. He works at the intersection of voice-interface intuition and AI technical approaches, with a current focus on safer voice interfaces for AI assistants, wearables, robotics, and vehicles.
