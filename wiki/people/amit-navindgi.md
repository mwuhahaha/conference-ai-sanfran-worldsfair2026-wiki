## Biography
Amit Navindgi is a Senior Staff Software Engineer and AI lead at [[zoox|Zoox]], where he founded and leads [[zoox|Zoox]] Intelligence, a company-wide initiative applying Large Language Models across engineering, operations, customer support, autonomy, and employee productivity. His work combines platform engineering with organizational AI adoption. He architects internal AI platforms, agents, and developer productivity workflows, while also leading AI tool evaluation, rollout strategy, enablement, spend management, and productivity measurement across [[zoox|Zoox]]. Amit also runs the [[zoox|Zoox]] Hackathon and The Assembly, a cross-functional forum for knowledge sharing and innovation. Earlier in his career, he built web applications and distributed systems at Veritas Technologies and worked on Natural Language Processing at Xerox Research Centre Europe.
