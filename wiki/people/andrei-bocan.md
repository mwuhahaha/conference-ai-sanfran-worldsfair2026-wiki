# Andrei Bocan

## Profile
Andrei Bocan is a Principal Engineer at [[atlassian|Atlassian]] and co-presenter of a confirmed sponsor workshop on building an organization-specific software-delivery lifecycle through orchestration. With Shane Wolf, he connects [[software-factories|software factories]] to the practical coordination of coding agents, engineering workflows, context, and human review.

## Biography
Andrei Bocan is a Principal Engineer at [[atlassian|Atlassian]] whose broader work includes GraphQL, schema evolution, and platform/API architecture. At AI Engineer World’s Fair 2026, he joins Shane Wolf for the two-hour workshop “The best SDLC is the one you build yourself: Why orchestration changes everything.” The session frames [[software-factories|software factories]] as repeatable delivery systems that connect issue intake and context assembly with planning, implementation, and review, allowing teams to shape orchestration around their own software-development environment rather than adopt a single fixed SDLC.
