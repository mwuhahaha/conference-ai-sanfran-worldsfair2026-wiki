# Andrew Garvin

## Official Role
Cofounder of Metronome at Stripe.

## Official Bio
Andrew Garvin is co-founder of Metronome, now part of Stripe. Andrew began his career at Peter Thiel’s hedge fund, working with Palantir in the early days, and then built his career as a startup and venture CFO in the Founders Fund network.
