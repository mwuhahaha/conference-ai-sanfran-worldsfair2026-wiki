## Biography
Andrew is the Chief of Software at [[vercel|Vercel]], where he leads the company's agent initiatives across product, infrastructure, and internal tooling in the Office of the CTO. He's the creator of skills.sh, the most popular way to discover and install new agent skills, and is building "an agent on every desk" inside [[vercel|Vercel]]. The most prominent so far is a data science agent that fields 2,000+ questions a day from Vercelians across engineering, finance, and go-to-market. Before [[vercel|Vercel]], Andrew founded a Series B AI sales-tech company, and has worked at Meta and early-stage startups alike.
