## Biography
Anil Nadiminti is a Senior Solutions Architect at AWS who helps Enterprise FinTech and Web3 customers design secure, scalable, production-ready cloud systems. His conference work connects that background to agentic commerce: autonomous agents that can discover paid services, authorize transactions, and compensate sellers through web-native protocols such as x402. His session, “When AI Agents Pay and Sellers Monetize,” focuses this work on building x402 applications on AWS and places payments alongside the other production boundaries agents must navigate, including tools, data, and interfaces exposed through protocols such as MCP. Within the conference graph, Nadiminti represents AWS’s programmable-commerce perspective and its practical concern with turning emerging agent standards into deployable architectures for buyers, sellers, APIs, and machine-to-machine services.
