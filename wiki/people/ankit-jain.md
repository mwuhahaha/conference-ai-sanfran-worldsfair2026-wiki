Ankit Jain is a founder and CEO of [[aviator|Aviator]], a developer productivity platform used by modern engineering teams to ship AI-generated code at scale — without the review bottlenecks, broken builds, or brittle deployments. He also leads The Hangar, a community of senior engineers and engineering leaders focused on developer experience, and Xoogler, the ex-Google alumni network.
