## Biography
Aparna Dhinakaran is the Co-Founder and Chief Product Officer at [[arize|Arize]] AI, a pioneer and early leader in AI & Agent observability and evaluation. A frequent speaker at top conferences and thought leader in the space, Dhinakaran was recently named to the Forbes 30 Under 30. Before [[arize|Arize]], Dhinakaran was an ML engineer and leader at Uber, Apple, and TubeMogul (acquired by Adobe). During her time at Uber, she built several core ML Infrastructure platforms, including Michealangelo. She has a bachelor’s from Berkeley's Electrical Engineering and Computer Science program, where she published research with Berkeley's AI Research group.
