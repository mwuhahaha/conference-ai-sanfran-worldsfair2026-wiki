# Benjamin Guo

## Official Role
Cofounder at Zo Computer.

## Official Bio
Cofounder of Zo Computer. Joined Stripe early (2015), where he worked for over 8 years. Founding engineer on Terminal, Stripe's in-person payments arm. Ben's cofounder, Rob Cheung, was the first engineer at Substack. They met on the early Venmo team in 2013, and they've reunited to build Zo.
