# Chaitanya Asawa

## Official Role
Head of Engineering for Clinical Decision Support at Abridge.

## Official Bio
Chaitanya leads agentic experiences & clinical decision support at Abridge, building the Jarvis for Clinicians. Previously he was one of the Founding Engineers at Glean where he built the Glean Assistant ground up technically and core teams. He started his career at Vicarious, an AI Research Lab focused on probabilistic methods & robotics.
