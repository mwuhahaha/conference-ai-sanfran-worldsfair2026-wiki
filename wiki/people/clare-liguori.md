## Biography
Clare Liguori is a Senior Principal Engineer at [[amazon-web-services|Amazon Web Services]] (AWS), where her agentic AI work centers on Kiro and the Strands Agents SDK. As a core maintainer of the Model Context Protocol (MCP) specification, she also contributes to the standards shaping how AI applications connect with tools and context. At AI Engineer World's Fair 2026, she brings that technical and organizational perspective to a session on building a frontier development team and progressing from AI-assisted software development to AI-native engineering.
