# Cormac Brick

## Official Role
Principal Engineer, Google AI Edge at Google.

## Official Bio
Principal Engineer at Google working on edge AI.  Lead on the Google AI Edge team and contributes to Gemma Model development.  An early pioneer in Edge AI, his industry background includes leading Intel’s first laptop NPUs, as well as demonstrating edge AI on a USB key way back in NeurIPS 2016.  These days he is technical lead for a team that drives multiple open source projects (Google AI Edge Gallery, LiteRT-LM, LiterRT, Xnnpack, Mediapipe) that enable Edge AI for billions of users.
