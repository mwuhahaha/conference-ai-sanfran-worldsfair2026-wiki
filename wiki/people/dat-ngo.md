# Dat Ngo

## Profile
Dat Ngo is an AI Architect at [[arize-ai|Arize AI]] whose work centers on agent evaluation, observability, experimentation, and the production harnesses needed to verify real outcomes rather than trust an agent’s self-reported success. His World’s Fair session places that problem in the context of shipping dependable AI systems, while linked platform material shows how evaluation pipelines and observability can support that verification at scale.

## Biography
Dat Ngo represents [[arize-ai|Arize AI]] in “Your Agent Is Lying to You About Whether It Worked,” a June 29 Expo Stage 1 session focused on the mismatch between an agent claiming completion and the system actually achieving its intended result. That concern connects directly to his broader work on measurable agent performance: defining success criteria, capturing production behavior, evaluating outcomes, and using experiments to improve reliability. The linked “LLM Observability, Evaluation, Experimentation Platform” video and its extracted, dense, and reconstructed slide sets provide supporting—not official event—context for the evaluation and observability infrastructure behind this approach.
