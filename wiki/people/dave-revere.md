# Dave Revere

## Official Role
Staff AI Engineer at SonderMind.

## Official Bio
Staff AI Engineer at SonderMind specializing in eval and guardrail pipelines for mental health AI. Dave builds the safety infrastructure behind SonderMind's AI products, including the clinical feedback loop that turns therapist annotations into automated regression tests and the modular guardrails system that evaluates every agent response before it reaches a user. He's passionate about making high-stakes AI systems safe enough to ship fast and rigorous enough to earn clinical trust.
