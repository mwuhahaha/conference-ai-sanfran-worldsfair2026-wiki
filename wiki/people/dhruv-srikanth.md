## Biography
Dhruv Srikanth is a Founding Engineer at [[weco-ai|Weco AI]], where he works on recursive self-improvement. His work includes AIDE, an autoresearch-style system that achieved nearly 4x the medal rate of the next-best autonomous agent across OpenAI’s MLE-Bench. Previously, Dhruv worked on computer vision and robotics research at Carnegie Mellon University and the University of Chicago.
