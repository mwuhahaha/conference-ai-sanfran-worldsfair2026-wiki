## Official Bio
Dominik Kundel works on Developer Experience at [[openai|OpenAI]], where he helps builders get the most out of Codex and the [[openai|OpenAI]] APIs. His work has spanned the Agents SDK, GPT-OSS, and most recently Codex. Before [[openai|OpenAI]], he led Product & Design for Twilio’s Emerging Tech & Innovation team, working on developer tools and customer-aware AI agents. Dominik has spent more than a decade in developer tools, usually across APIs, CLIs, JavaScript, and strange demos. Outside work, he’s probably tinkering with cocktails, food, photography, or something that should not need JavaScript but somehow does.
