## Biography
Edo Liberty is the founder and Chief Scientist of [[pinecone|Pinecone]]. [[pinecone|Pinecone]] is the knowledge infrastructure for AI at scale. Its leading vector database and knowledge engine, [[pinecone|Pinecone]] Nexus, power accurate, performant AI applications for more than 10,000 customers and 1M developers worldwide.  Before founding [[pinecone|Pinecone]], Edo was a Director of Research at AWS and Head of Amazon AI Labs where his team built cutting-edge machine learning algorithms, systems, and services including parts of Amazon SageMaker and OpenSearch. Edo holds a B.Sc in Physics and Computer Science from Tel Aviv University, and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Yale. As an academic Edo taught at Tel Aviv University and at Princeton and has authored more than 75 papers and patents. His research focused on mathematical foundations of AI, optimization, streaming algorithms, machine learning, numerical linear algebra, and high dimensional data mining.
