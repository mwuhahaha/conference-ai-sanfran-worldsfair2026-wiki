## Biography
Eyal Blum is a senior staff engineer at [[figma|Figma]] working on Client Testing, Observability, and Performance. He works on developer infrastructure and drives AI-assisted development practices across the engineering org, sitting at the intersection of developer tooling and AI adoption — figuring out what it takes for coding agents to actually work in a large, established codebase. Before [[figma|Figma]], Eyal spent nearly 20 years building large-scale systems at companies like Meta, Dropbox, and Google.
