# Gabriel Spencer-Harper

## Official Role
CEO at Meticulous.

## Official Bio
Gabriel Spencer-Harper is CEO and co-founder of Meticulous, which builds AI-powered UI testing tooling to eliminate the need to write and maintain UI tests. He previously worked in engineering at Dropbox and Opendoor.
