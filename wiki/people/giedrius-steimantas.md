# Giedrius Steimantas

## Profile
Giedrius Steimantas is Director of Scraping Engineering at [[oxylabs|Oxylabs]], where he leads engineering behind scraper APIs and the reliable, large-scale collection of public web data. At AI Engineer World's Fair 2026, his work is represented as infrastructure for agentic systems that need dependable access to current information from the public web.

- [LinkedIn](https://lt.linkedin.com/in/steimantas)

## Biography
Steimantas represents [[oxylabs|Oxylabs]] alongside Product Manager Patricija Žemaitytė, whose conference focus includes SERP and web-data products. His Expo Stage 1 NE session, [[2026-07-01-giedrius-steimantas-the-missing-layer-in-agentic-ai|The Missing Layer in Agentic AI]], draws on his leadership of scraping engineering and scraper API teams to examine the web-data layer required by AI agents: infrastructure capable of retrieving public information reliably and at scale rather than treating web access as an incidental capability.
