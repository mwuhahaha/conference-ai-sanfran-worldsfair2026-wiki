# Gus Iwanaga

## Official Role
General Manager (Product, UX, Eng) at commercetools.

## Official Bio
Gus Iwanaga is founder and GM for mosAIc at commercetools, where he works on agentic commerce.  Before commercetools, Gus held senior product roles at Google Shopping, Zalando, and zooplus, building  products used by millions of shoppers & merchants across Europe.
