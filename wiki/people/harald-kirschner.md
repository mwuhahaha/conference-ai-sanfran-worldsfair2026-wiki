## Profile

His two World’s Fair appearances focus on a concrete operating challenge: how a roughly 40-person VS Code team coordinates decisions, release practices, and continuous improvement while shipping every week. The separately linked presentation “Real World Development with GitHub Copilot and VS Code,” co-presented with Christopher Harrison, provides supporting—not official 2026 event—context for the developer experience produced by that cadence.
