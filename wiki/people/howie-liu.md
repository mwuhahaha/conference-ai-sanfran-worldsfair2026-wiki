## Biography
Howie is the CEO of [[airtable|Airtable]], which he co-founded in 2013 with the vision of creating a radically faster and more intuitive way to build useful applications. Since launch, [[airtable|Airtable]] has gained a groundswell of adoption across a range of customers from small businesses to the world's largest enterprises, with over 500,000 organizations using [[airtable|Airtable]]'s AI-native platform to build applications that fit their workflows. Now counting over half of the Fortune 500 as paid customers, [[airtable|Airtable]] enables organizations to turn the power of AI into measurable business impact and power mission-critical enterprise use cases. The company recently launched Superagent, a standalone product built on multi-agent coordination that delivers research and analysis through teams of specialized AI agents working in parallel. Headquartered in San Francisco, [[airtable|Airtable]] has raised $1.36 billion to date and employs over 750 employees.
