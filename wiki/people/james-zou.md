## Official Bio
James Zou is an associate professor of Biomedical Data Science at Stanford, with courtesy appointments in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering, and a Stanford HAI faculty affiliate. His AI for Science work includes collective AI-agent systems for scientific discovery with Together AI.
