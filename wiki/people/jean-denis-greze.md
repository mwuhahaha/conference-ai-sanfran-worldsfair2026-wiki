# Jean-Denis Greze

## Official Role
Co-Founder & CEO at Town.

## Official Bio
Jean-Denis Greze is co-founder and CEO of Town, a personal AI assistant that does real work for people inside the tools they already use - email, calendar, Slack, and more. Previously he was Chief Technology Officer at Plaid, where he led engineering through the company's hypergrowth, and before that a Director of Engineering at Dropbox. He also invests through ASDF Ventures. He's spoken and written widely on engineering leadership and building "spiky" organizations, and is based in San Francisco.
