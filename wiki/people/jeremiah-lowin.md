## Biography
Jeremiah Lowin is the founder and CEO of [[prefect|Prefect]] and the creator of FastMCP. [[prefect|Prefect]] builds automation and orchestration tools used by teams working across data, AI, and software infrastructure. A former quantitative researcher, Jeremiah has spent his career designing systems that make complex work observable, dependable, and easier to reason about. Before founding [[prefect|Prefect]], he led risk and data initiatives for major investment firms and was a founding member of the Apache Airflow PMC. He now advises companies like Spotify on technology strategy. His current work focuses on the infrastructure beneath AI applications: orchestration, tools, protocols, and the practical details that turn interesting demos into reliable software. Jeremiah holds bachelor's and master's degrees from Harvard University and lives in Washington, DC.
