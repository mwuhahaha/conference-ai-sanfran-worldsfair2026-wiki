# Jeremiah Lowin

## Official Role
Founder & CEO at Prefect.

## Official Bio
Jeremiah Lowin is the founder and CEO of Prefect and the creator of FastMCP. Prefect builds automation and orchestration tools used by teams working across data, AI, and software infrastructure. A former quantitative researcher, Jeremiah has spent his career designing systems that make complex work observable, dependable, and easier to reason about. Before founding Prefect, he led risk and data initiatives for major investment firms and was a founding member of the Apache Airflow PMC. He now advises companies like Spotify on technology strategy. His current work focuses on the infrastructure beneath AI applications: orchestration, tools, protocols, and the practical details that turn interesting demos into reliable software. Jeremiah holds bachelor's and master's degrees from Harvard University and lives in Washington, DC.
