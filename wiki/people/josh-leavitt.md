## Official Bio
Josh Leavitt is Senior Director of Product Management at Coinbase, where he leads AI Platform strategy and innovation. Josh oversees core initiatives aimed at making AI more accessible and secure at Coinbase. Prior to joining Coinbase, he held leadership roles at Amazon Web Services. He is dedicated to building secure, accessible financial infrastructure, focusing on large-scale platform growth.
