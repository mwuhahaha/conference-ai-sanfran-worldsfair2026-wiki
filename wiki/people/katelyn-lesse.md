## Official Bio
Katelyn Lesse is the Head of Platform Engineering at Anthropic. She leads engineering for the Claude Platform, including APIs and developer tooling, as well as Anthropic’s product infrastructure. She has experience building and scaling engineering organizations across fintech and developer platforms, with previous leadership roles at Stripe and Betterment.
