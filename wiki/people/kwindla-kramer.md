# Kwindla Kramer

## Profile
Kwindla Kramer is CEO and co-founder of [[daily|Daily]], where his work focuses on real-time voice infrastructure and the software primitives needed to build responsive AI applications.

- [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kwkramer/)

## Biography
Kramer is a contributor to Pipecat and an advocate for treating voice, real-time media, model interaction, and tool connectivity as composable foundations for AI-native software. His two World’s Fair sessions span that architecture-to-interface arc: [[2026-06-29-kwindla-kramer-the-new-primitives-building-ai-native-software|The New Primitives: Building AI-Native Software]] examines the building blocks behind AI-native systems, while [[2026-07-01-kwindla-kramer-voice-is-the-universal-interface|Voice is the universal interface]], presented with Neil Zeghidour, focuses on speech as a general interaction layer. Supporting conference media also connects him to practical constraints in real-time AI through “Your realtime AI is ngmi,” a discussion with Sean DuBois.
