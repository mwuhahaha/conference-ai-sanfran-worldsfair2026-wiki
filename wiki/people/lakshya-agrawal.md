# Lakshya Agrawal

## Official Role
Creator and maintainer of GEPA at GEPA.

## Official Bio
Lakshya A. Agrawal is the creator and maintainer of GEPA and a second-year EECS PhD student at UC Berkeley’s Sky Computing Lab. His research focuses on optimization, evaluation, and self-improvement for LLM-based agents and systems. He previously worked as an AI4Code Research Fellow at Microsoft Research.
