## Biography
Leo is a Director of Engineering at [[ramp|Ramp]], where he's built the Forward Deployed Engineering function (ramp.com/fde) and also makes [[ramp|Ramp]] usable by agents (agents.ramp.com). He previously was co-founder and head of eng at cybersecurity startup Lumos (backed by a16z and Neo). Leo completed an MS in CS at Stanford and lives in SF.
