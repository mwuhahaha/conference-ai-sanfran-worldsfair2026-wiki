## Biography
Maxime builds tools and create content that make LLMs more accessible and powerful for everyone. He is a core contributor to [[dspy|DSPy]] and has built numerous open-source Python libraries to advance the ecosystem, including attachments, functai, ovllm, dspy-lm-auth, dspy-template-adapter, and mcp2py. Previously, he worked at Elsevier, building AI infrastructure and compound AI programs to cost effectively run on 100 million records weekly.
