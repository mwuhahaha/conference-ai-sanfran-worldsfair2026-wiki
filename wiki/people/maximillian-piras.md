# Maximillian Piras

## Official Role
Founding Designer at Yutori.

## Official Bio
Currently focused on UIUX for agentic AI as the Founding Designer at Yutori. Previously Head of Design at Headliner and Sr. Designer at 8tracks, leading cross-platform UIUX for millions of users. Additionally, developed graphics/animations for clients including Giphy, MIT, & Ryuichi Sakamoto while also being a contributing writer to Smashing Magazine.
