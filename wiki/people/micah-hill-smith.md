## Profile

At AI Engineer World’s Fair 2026, he brings this cross-market measurement perspective to the Autoresearch main-stage keynote “Trends in AI,” connecting comparative benchmark evidence with the practical engineering cycle of testing bounded changes and measuring their results.

## Biography
Micah Hill-Smith leads [[artificial-analysis|Artificial Analysis]], developing public benchmarks, leaderboards, and evaluation frameworks that help engineers compare models, agents, API providers, inference systems, and hardware on concrete performance dimensions. Its analysis is cited by frontier AI organizations including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and NVIDIA. In [[2026-06-30-george-cameron-trends-in-ai|Trends in AI]], Hill-Smith joins George Cameron for a confirmed Autoresearch main-stage keynote that places market-wide AI trends in the context of [[autoresearch|Autoresearch]]: holding a research objective steady, gathering evidence, proposing a bounded intervention, measuring its effect against a baseline, and using the result to guide the next iteration.
