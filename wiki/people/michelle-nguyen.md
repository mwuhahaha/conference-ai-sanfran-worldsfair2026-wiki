## Biography
Michelle Nguyen is cofounder of [[gimlet-labs|Gimlet Labs]], the first multi-silicon inference cloud to run agentic workloads across different types of hardware, where she leads engineering. She was the first engineer at Pixie Labs where she worked across the stack on projects ranging from Pixie's deployment mechanisms to its distributed query engine. Before Pixie, Michelle was at Trifacta helping build intuitive and interactive UIs. Michelle holds a MS and BS in EECS from UC Berkeley.
