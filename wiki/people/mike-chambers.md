# Mike Chambers

## Profile
Mike Chambers is a Senior Developer Advocate for Generative AI at [[amazon-web-services-aws|Amazon Web Services (AWS)]]. His work focuses on moving agentic systems from demonstrations into production through practical harnesses, operational safeguards, and repeatable engineering workflows.

- [Blog](https://blog.mikegchambers.com)

## Biography
Chambers creates hands-on educational material about agentic AI and Amazon Bedrock, including serverless agent workflows and Generative AI with Large Language Models. At World’s Fair 2026, he brings that production-oriented perspective to harness engineering: the surrounding “cage” that gives powerful domain agents the context, constraints, evaluation, and operational structure needed for real-world applications. His linked supporting talk, “Ship it! Building Production Ready Agents,” reinforces this emphasis and connects his work to the broader [[software-factories|software-factory]] model of repeatable agent delivery.
