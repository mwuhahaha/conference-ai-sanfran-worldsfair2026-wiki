## Biography
Head of AI & Research at [[jellyfish|Jellyfish]], building AI agents and data platforms that help software engineering organizations measure and navigate AI transformation at scale. Leveraging massive real-world data sets to study what's working (and what's not) about AI tool and agent use across the industry, and sharing these learnings through published research and benchmarks so engineering leaders can make confident, evidence-based decisions. Harvard Ph.D., previously at TrueMotion (CMT), Runkeeper, MIT Lincoln Laboratory.
