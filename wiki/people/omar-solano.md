## Biography
Omar Solano is an AI Engineer at [[towards-ai|Towards AI]], where he architects and builds production AI agents and applied LLM systems. His work spans RAG, fine-tuning, agentic workflows, and long-context and reasoning-model systems. He leads client-facing AI consulting projects and delivers hands-on AI engineering workshops for developers, engineering teams, and international conference audiences, including training for Europol and the New York Public Library. Omar has authored 50+ technical lessons and book chapters on RAG, AI agents, fine-tuning, and coding agents, reaching 90,000+ learners through [[towards-ai|Towards AI]]'s courses and publications.
