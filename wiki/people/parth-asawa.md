# Parth Asawa

## Official Role
CS PhD student at UC Berkeley.

## Official Bio
Parth Asawa is a PhD student at UC Berkeley advised by Professor Matei Zaharia and Professor Joey Gonzalez. Parth's research is on continual learning, studying how to enable models to stably learn from streams of experiences over time. His work focuses on sample-efficient learning and spans the stack of data, learning algorithms, architectures, and evaluation.
