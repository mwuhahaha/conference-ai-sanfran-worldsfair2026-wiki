# Rob Wachen

## Official Role
Co-founder and President at Etched.

## Official Bio
Rob Wachen is the co-founder and president of Etched. Etched is building rack-scale infrastructure designed to serve frontier models at scale. A Thiel Fellow and Harvard dropout, Rob previously co-founded Prod, a startup accelerator with a cohort valuation of $100B+.
