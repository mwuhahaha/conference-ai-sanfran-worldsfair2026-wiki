## Biography
Sean Singh Sodha is a deep learning Senior Product Manager at [[nvidia|NVIDIA]], responsible for the Nemotron Retriever portfolio of embedding, reranking, and extraction models powering agentic retrieval and memory systems. Before joining [[nvidia|NVIDIA]], Sean ran his own Generative AI venture and was formerly at IBM Watson. He holds an MBA from the Wharton School of Business, M.S. in engineering from Cornell University, and B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Purdue University.
