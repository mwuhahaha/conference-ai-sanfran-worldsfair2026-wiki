## Official Bio
Suchet Bargoti is Director of Inspection and Mapping at Skydio, where he leads work on autonomous drone systems for capturing, inspecting, and understanding critical infrastructure. His work spans field robotics, perception, and applied autonomy, with a focus on making drones reliable tools for collecting high-quality data in complex real-world environments.
