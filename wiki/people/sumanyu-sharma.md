# Sumanyu Sharma

## Official Role
Founder/CEO at Hamming AI.

## Official Bio
Sumanyu Sharma is the Founder & CEO of Hamming AI, a YC company that invented automated testing, monitoring, and red-teaming for AI voice agents. Hamming helps teams catch issues before their agents confidently say the wrong thing to a real customer. Before Hamming, Sumanyu worked on high-stakes systems at Tesla and Citizen across revenue, safety, and emergency response. He started in AI research at Waterloo, teaching models to search X-rays by meaning. Put simply, he has spent his career turning “the AI seems fine” into “the AI passed the test".
