## Biography
Tanmay Sah, PhD, is a quantitative modeler and AI researcher working at the intersection of predictive modeling, model risk, AI evaluation, and agentic AI systems. His notable work includes research on AI agent verification; TanML, an open-source automated machine learning model validation toolkit; and Decoding Reddit Memes Virality. He is especially interested in the next generation of trustworthy AI systems: agents that can reason, use tools, remain auditable, and operate safely under real-world constraints.
