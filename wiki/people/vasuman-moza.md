# Vasuman Moza

## Official Role
Founder & CEO at Varick Agents.

## Official Bio
Vas Moza is the founder and CEO of Varick Agents, a San Francisco startup transforming enterprise companies with AI. Varick's approach starts with an audit: the team embeds with each customer to map how the business actually works, then ships the agents, integrations, and workflows that take AI from prototype to dependable production. As one part of that mission, Varick is building the AI FDE: an agent that does the work of a forward-deployed engineer - scoping problems, writing and integrating code, and standing up production systems alongside the people who run them. Varick is built on the bet that the hard part of enterprise AI isn't the model - it's deployment, reliability, and trust.
