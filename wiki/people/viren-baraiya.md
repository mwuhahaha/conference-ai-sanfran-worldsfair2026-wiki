# Viren Baraiya

## Official Role
Co-Founder and CTO at Orkes.

## Official Bio
Viren Baraiya is Co-Founder and CTO of Orkes, a company building cloud services around the Conductor orchestration platform. He was one of the original creators of Netflix Conductor and previously led engineering work at Netflix and Google.
