## Official Bio
Vivek leads Applied Research at LangChain Labs where he's focused on cracking Continual Learning & making it accessible to the world's agent builders.  Previously he worked on the LangChain Deep Agents open-source agent harness, worked on his own startup around agents for visual reasoning, and did Health AI at AWS for ~4 years. His PhD was focused on representation learning in Computer Vision.
