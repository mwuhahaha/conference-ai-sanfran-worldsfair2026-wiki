## Biography
Yuchen Fama is a Builder, Benchmarker, and Senior Principal Product Manager of Inference at [[red-hat|Red Hat]] and also a contributor to vLLM and GuideLLM. She has more than 15 years of experience in ML and AI. She has served as VP of Product, CTO, and CPO at multiple AI startups and previously led AI/ML research teams within several Fortune 500 companies. She holds a Ph.D. in Statistics and enjoys reading, traveling, skiing, and scuba diving.
